// Who we are

We're a small, scrappy collective that treats constraints as a feature — turning limited resources into focused, standalone software that just works.

Our mission. Build practical, self-contained digital products — micro-apps, developer plugins, and localized infrastructure — that solve real problems without the bloat of heavy platforms or vendor lock-in.

Our vision. Prove that a lean, indie-hacker approach can ship production-grade tooling. Every repo is a bet on craftsmanship over scale, and on engineering grit over shortcuts.

Standalone by design

Products that run on their own — no sprawling dependencies.

Systems-level craft

Shells, daemons & native tooling in C, C++, QML.

Open by default

Everything we build lives in the open on GitHub.

Locally rooted

Built in India, focused on localized infrastructure.
